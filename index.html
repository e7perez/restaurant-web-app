NYC Rooftop Bar & Grill

About Us

NYC Rooftop Bar & Grill is New York's largest outdoor Rooftop Garden and Fully enclosed Penthouse Lounge. One floor is fully enclosed. And our rooftop garden is open to the sky. We do have large umbrellas for those sunny or rainy days and is partially heated on colder nights.

COVID-19 Guidelines

All guests MUST wear a mask at all times while in the establishment. This includes before and after being seated and while standing. Guests can remove their masks ONLY while after being seated, eating and drinking.
Guests must maintain 6 ft social distancing at all times, including while seated.
All guests must have a physical, non-expired government issued ID or Passport. All guests must be 21+ years of age or accompanied by a parent up until 7PM, and out by 8PM.
No outside food or beverages, including water bottles. No weapons or illegal drugs will be permitted.
No more than 10 guests per group. All members of party must order food.
